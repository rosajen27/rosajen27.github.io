The Tasty Burger

A restaurant app that allows the user to order the burger they’d like to eat. When the user submits a burger’s name, the app will display the burger on the left side of the page, waiting to be devoured. When the order is ready, the burger will move to the right side of the page. This app will store every burger in a database, whether devoured or not.

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, MySQL, Heroku, Handlebars
